## Overview

This guide explains the confidentiality expectations for all developers, contributors, testers, and staff members who are given access to internal PulseGrid Studios projects.

The purpose of these guidelines is to protect:

- Unreleased game content
- Scripts and systems
- Internal plans
- Development assets
- Private staff discussions
- Community and player information

> ℹ️ **Note**  
> Anyone with access to private development areas is trusted to handle information responsibly.

---

## Purpose of the NDA

The Developer NDA exists to make sure that private project information remains secure.

Developers may be given access to systems, assets, documents, or discussions that are not public. This information must not be shared outside approved staff or development areas.

The NDA helps protect the project from:

- Leaks
- Asset theft
- Code misuse
- False information being spread
- Unauthorised previews
- Damage to the community or studio reputation

> 💡 **Tip**  
> If something has not been officially announced, treat it as confidential.

---

## Confidential Information

Confidential information includes, but is not limited to:

- Unreleased scripts or systems
- Roblox Studio files or places
- Internal game mechanics
- Development plans or roadmaps
- Staff-only documents
- Private Discord channels
- Test builds or screenshots
- Unreleased UI, models, maps, or assets
- Security systems or anti-exploit methods
- Player reports, moderation logs, or private user information
- Internal discussions between developers or staff

> ⚠️ **Warning**  
> Even small leaks can cause major issues for the project.

---

## Developer Responsibilities

Developers must:

- Keep all private information confidential
- Only access files and systems needed for their role
- Use project assets only for authorised development work
- Report security issues or accidental leaks immediately
- Ask for permission before sharing previews
- Respect other developers’ work
- Keep internal discussions private
- Follow all studio, Discord, and Roblox rules

> 💡 **Tip**  
> When unsure whether something can be shared, ask a senior staff member first.

---

## What Developers Must Not Do

Developers must not:

- Share private scripts, assets, or files
- Leak screenshots, videos, or previews
- Copy project assets for personal use
- Reupload or reuse studio content elsewhere
- Share internal plans with non-staff members
- Give access to unauthorised users
- Discuss private development matters publicly
- Use project systems to gain unfair advantages
- Claim ownership of work they did not create
- Sell, trade, or distribute studio assets

> ⚠️ **Warning**  
> Sharing confidential content without permission may result in immediate removal from the development team.

---

## Access to Project Assets

Access to development files is granted based on trust and role requirements.

Developers may only use project files for official PulseGrid Studios work. Access may be limited, changed, or removed at any time if required.

Examples of controlled access include:

- Roblox Studio team create permissions
- Private Discord channels
- Internal documents
- Development builds
- Scripts and modules
- Asset libraries
- Testing environments

> ℹ️ **Note**  
> Access is a privilege, not a permanent right.

---

## Sharing Previews

Developers must not post previews of unreleased content unless permission has been given.

This includes:

- Screenshots
- Videos
- Clips
- Script previews
- UI previews
- Model previews
- Feature announcements

Only approved announcements should be shared publicly.

> 💡 **Tip**  
> Public previews should come from official PulseGrid Studios channels whenever possible.

---

## Leaving the Development Team

When a developer leaves the team, they must:

- Stop using all private project files
- Remove any local copies if requested
- Keep all previously accessed information confidential
- Not share internal information after leaving
- Not reuse private assets or scripts in other projects
- Return or transfer any required work files

Leaving the team does not remove confidentiality expectations.

> ⚠️ **Warning**  
> Former developers are still expected to respect private project information.

---

## Breaches of Confidentiality

A breach of confidentiality may include:

- Leaking private content
- Sharing scripts or assets
- Giving access to unauthorised users
- Posting unreleased information
- Misusing internal tools
- Copying project work without permission

Possible actions may include:

### 1. Access Removal
Development access may be removed immediately.

### 2. Staff Review
Senior staff may review the incident and available evidence.

### 3. Removal from Team
The developer may be removed from the project permanently.

### 4. Server Action
Discord moderation action may be taken if server rules were broken.

### 5. Roblox Report
Serious asset theft or misuse may be reported through Roblox where appropriate.

> ℹ️ **Note**  
> Not all incidents are intentional, but all incidents must be reported quickly.

---

## Developer Conduct

Developers are representatives of PulseGrid Studios and should act professionally.

Developers should:

- Communicate respectfully
- Avoid drama or public arguments
- Keep criticism constructive
- Respect deadlines and expectations
- Be honest about progress
- Ask for help when needed
- Avoid damaging the reputation of the project

> 💡 **Tip**  
> Professional conduct helps build trust within the team.

---

## Final Notes

The Developer NDA is about protecting the project, the team, and the community.

Developers are trusted with private access and are expected to act responsibly at all times.

> ⚠️ **Warning**  
> If you are unsure whether something is confidential, assume that it is.

---

## Agreement

By accepting a developer, contributor, tester, or staff role with access to private project areas, you agree to follow these confidentiality guidelines.

Failure to follow these guidelines may result in removal from the development team and loss of access to PulseGrid Studios projects.
