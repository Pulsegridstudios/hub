## Overview

This page allows contributors to submit new wiki pages or updates.

The process is simple:
1. Download the template
2. Write your page
3. Upload it for review
4. A team member will review and publish it

---

## 📥 Step 1 — Download Template

Use the official template to ensure your page matches the wiki format.

- 📄 [Download Page Template](https://cloud.grants-nextcloud.co.uk/public.php/dav/files/pNnLf2yz6Sab9eF/?accept=zip)
- 📘 [Markdown Guide](https://cloud.grants-nextcloud.co.uk/public.php/dav/files/qy3KaZsJta9caQS/?accept=zip)
- 🧭 [Style Guide](https://cloud.grants-nextcloud.co.uk/public.php/dav/files/p8im4rPg5s7Tczb/?accept=zip)

> 💡 **Tip**  
> Always start from the template to avoid formatting issues.

---

## ✍️ Step 2 — Write Your Page

Create your page using the template.

Make sure to:
- include correct frontmatter
- use headings and sections
- add tips/warnings where appropriate
- link related pages

---

## 📤 Step 3 — Upload Your Page

When finished, upload your file using the link below:

- 📤 [Upload Wiki Submission](https://cloud.grants-nextcloud.co.uk/index.php/s/5PNaG3xsMY7o3zi)

> ℹ️ **Note**  
> You can upload `.md` files and any supporting images.

---

## 🧪 Submission Checklist

Before uploading, please check:

- [ ] Page uses the official template  
- [ ] Title and permalink are correct  
- [ ] `nav_group` is set correctly  
- [ ] Formatting is clean and readable  
- [ ] Links work correctly  
- [ ] Images are included (if needed)  

---

## ⚠️ Important Notes

> ⚠️ **Warning**  
> Submissions may be edited for formatting, clarity, or consistency before being published.

> ℹ️ **Note**  
> Not all submissions will be accepted. Quality and accuracy are required.

---

## 🕒 Review Process

After submission:
- A team member reviews your page
- Edits may be applied if needed
- The page is added to the wiki

Typical review time:
- 1–3 days (depending on workload)

---

## 💬 Need Help?

If you're unsure about anything:
- Contact a team member
- Ask in the Discord
- Refer to the guides above

---

## 🚀 Final Note

> 💡 **Tip**  
> Clear, structured, and accurate pages are much more likely to be accepted quickly.
