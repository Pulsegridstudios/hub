## Overview

This guide defines how moderators should operate **within the game environment**.

The objective is to maintain:

- Fair gameplay  
- System integrity  
- A safe and structured environment  

> ℹ️ **Note**  
> In-game moderation has direct impact on gameplay and must be handled carefully.

---

## Moderator Responsibilities

Game moderators are responsible for:

- Enforcing rules within the game  
- Monitoring player behaviour  
- Responding to incidents  
- Preventing exploitation or abuse  

---

## Moderator Conduct

Moderators must:

- Remain neutral and unbiased  
- Avoid interfering with normal gameplay unnecessarily  
- Never use tools for personal advantage  
- Communicate clearly and professionally  

> ⚠️ **Warning**  
> Abuse of in-game permissions will result in immediate removal.

---

## Moderation Tools

Moderators may have access to:

- Kick / Ban systems  
- Warning systems  
- Spectate tools  
- Logs and player data  
- Admin panels  

These tools must only be used for **legitimate moderation purposes**.

---

## Moderation Actions

Moderation should follow a structured approach:

### 1. Observation
Assess the situation before acting.

### 2. Warning
Inform the player of the issue.

### 3. Action
Apply appropriate moderation (kick, timeout, etc.)

### 4. Escalation
Escalate serious or repeated issues.

---

## Common Scenarios

### Exploiting

- Using scripts or unintended mechanics  

**Action:** Immediate kick or ban depending on severity  

---

### Disruptive Behaviour

- Interfering with gameplay or systems  

**Action:** Warning → Kick → Ban  

---

### Abuse of Systems

- Misuse of reactor controls or critical systems  

**Action:** Immediate intervention and escalation  

---

### Staff Abuse

- Misuse of moderator tools  

**Action:** Immediate removal and report  

> ⚠️ **Warning**  
> Staff abuse is treated as a critical violation.

---

## Abuse of Systems

The following is strictly prohibited:

- Using admin tools for personal benefit  
- Targeting players unfairly  
- Bypassing systems or safeguards  
- Altering gameplay without reason  

---

## Logging & Evidence

All moderation actions should be:

- Logged where possible  
- Supported with evidence  
- Reviewable by senior staff  

> 💡 **Tip**  
> If you cannot justify the action, it should not be taken.

---

## Final Notes

Game moderation should prioritise:

- Fairness  
- Stability  
- Consistency  

> 💡 **Tip**  
> The goal is to maintain the environment — not control it.
