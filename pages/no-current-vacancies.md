## Why Applications May Be Closed

Applications may be temporarily unavailable for several reasons, including:

- all current roles are filled
- the team is reviewing previous applications
- systems or training materials are being updated
- staffing is being paused while other priorities are handled

## What You Can Do In The Meantime

While applications are closed, you can still:

- stay active in the community
- follow project updates
- read contributor and moderation guides
- prepare for future openings

> 💡 **Tip**  
> Being active, helpful, and professional in the community can help when roles reopen.
