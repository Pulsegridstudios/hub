# Code of Conduct

## Overview

This section defines the expected standards of behaviour across Looped Operations.

It applies to:

- Players  
- Contributors  
- Moderators  
- Staff  

The goal is to maintain a **professional, fair, and structured environment** across all platforms.

---

## Scope

The Code of Conduct covers:

- Discord moderation  
- In-game moderation  
- Staff behaviour and responsibilities  

---

## Core Principles

All individuals are expected to:

- Act professionally and respectfully  
- Follow established rules and procedures  
- Avoid disruptive or harmful behaviour  
- Respect staff decisions and systems  

---

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:

- Warnings  
- Restrictions  
- Removal from systems or platforms  

Severity depends on the nature of the violation.

---

## Moderation Guidelines

Detailed moderation procedures are outlined in the following sections:

- [Discord Moderation]({{ '/pages/discord-moderation/' | relative_url }})
- [Game Moderation]({{ '/pages/game-moderation/' | relative_url }})

---

## Staff Expectations

Staff members are held to a higher standard and must:

- Act impartially  
- Avoid abuse of systems  
- Follow internal procedures  
- Maintain professionalism at all times  

> ⚠️ **Warning**  
> Abuse of staff privileges will result in immediate removal.

---

## Final Note

> ℹ️ **Note**  
> The purpose of this system is to ensure consistency, fairness, and a stable environment — not to restrict normal interaction.
