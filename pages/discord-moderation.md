## Overview

Moderators are responsible for maintaining a safe and professional environment.

---

## Responsibilities

- Enforce server rules  
- Handle reports and tickets  
- Support users  
- Maintain order in discussions  

---

## Guidelines

- Remain neutral and professional  
- Do not abuse permissions  
- Follow escalation procedures  
- Log all actions where required  

---

## Important

> ⚠️ **Warning**  
> All moderation actions are logged and reviewed.
