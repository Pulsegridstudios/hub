## Overview

This guide defines how moderators should manage and maintain the Somewhat Operational Plant Discord server.

The goal is to create a **professional, structured, and safe environment** for:
- Community members
- Contributors
- Staff
- Players

> ℹ️ **Note**  
> Moderators are representatives of PulseGrid Studios and must act accordingly.4 messages have been deleted.

---

## Moderator Responsibilities

Moderators are responsible for:

- Enforcing rules fairly and consistently  
- Maintaining a calm and professional environment  
- Assisting users when needed  
- Escalating serious issues  

> 💡 **Tip**  
> Most moderation should be preventative — not reactive.

---

## Moderator Conduct

Moderators must:

- Remain professional at all times  
- Avoid arguments with users  
- Never abuse moderation tools  
- Keep internal discussions private  

> ⚠️ **Warning**  
> Abuse of moderation systems will result in immediate removal.

---

## Moderation Actions

Moderation should follow a **clear escalation path**:

### 1. Clarification
Explain the rule to prevent escalation.

### 2. Warning
Formal notice that behaviour must stop.

### 3. Message Removal
Delete inappropriate or harmful content.

### 4. Timeout
Temporary restriction from messaging.

### 5. Kick
Removal from server (can rejoin).

### 6. Ban
Permanent removal from server.

> ℹ️ **Note**  
> Not all situations require every step — use judgement.

---

## Common Scenarios

### Spam

- Repeated messages or flooding chat  

**Action:** Warning → Timeout → Ban

---

### Harassment / Bullying

- Targeting or attacking users  

**Action:** Immediate Warning or Timeout  
Escalate if continued  

> ⚠️ **Warning**  
> Severe harassment may result in instant bans.

---

### Hate Speech

- Racism, homophobia, etc.

**Action:** Immediate Ban

---

### Exploits / Cheating Discussion

- Sharing or promoting exploits  

**Action:** Warning → Ban  

---

### Staff Impersonation

- Pretending to be staff  

**Action:** Immediate Ban  

---

### False Reports

- Abuse of reporting systems  

**Action:** Warning → Escalation  

---

### Ticket Abuse

- Spamming or misusing support tickets  

**Action:** Warning → Timeout  

---

## Staff Rules

All staff must follow additional rules:

- Do not moderate when personally involved  
- Do not threaten punishments  
- Always log major actions  
- Escalate serious incidents  

> 💡 **Tip**  
> If unsure — escalate instead of guessing.

---

## Appeals Process

Users may appeal punishments through official channels.

Appeals should:

- Be reviewed fairly  
- Include evidence where possible  
- Be handled by staff not involved in the original action  

> ℹ️ **Note**  
> Not all bans are eligible for appeal.

---

## Final Notes

Moderation is about maintaining **order and professionalism**, not punishment.

> 💡 **Tip**  
> The best moderation is the kind users don’t notice.
